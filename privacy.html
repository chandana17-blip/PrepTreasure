Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At PrepTreasure, we prioritize the privacy of our students. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by PrepTreasure and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you register on our platform, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Identification: Name and Class.
  • Contact Information: Mobile Number (used for OTP verification and account recovery).
  • Device Information: Device ID and browser details to enforce our Single Device Login policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our educational services.
  • Verify your identity via OTP and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Track your quiz scores and leaderboard rankings.
  • Analyze website usage to improve user experience.

3. Data Security & Encryption

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored on secure cloud servers (Firebase) and is protected against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Services (Google AdSense)

We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your visit to this and other websites on the internet. You may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

5. Cookies and Web Beacons

PrepTreasure uses 'cookies' to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience.

6. Children's Privacy

As an educational platform for students, we ensure that the data collected is used strictly for academic progress tracking and is never shared with external marketing agencies.

7. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
